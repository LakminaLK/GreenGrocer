Our Branches

Kandy Branch

Located in the heart of the historic city, our Kandy branch offers a wide selection of fresh produce, dairy products, and grocery essentials. The vibrant atmosphere and friendly staff make shopping a pleasant experience. We are proud to source many of our vegetables from the nearby Kandy Main Market, ensuring freshness and quality.

Colombo Branch

Situated in the bustling city of Colombo, our branch caters to urban dwellers seeking high-quality groceries and exceptional service. With a broad range of products and convenient home delivery services, we aim to make grocery shopping a breeze. Our Colombo branch is known for its extensive range of international and local products, catering to a diverse clientele.

Kegalle Branch

Nestled in the serene town of Kegalle, this branch offers a cozy shopping experience with a focus on fresh and organic products. The Kegalle branch sources many of its vegetables from local farms, ensuring that our customers receive the freshest produce available. Friendly staff and a community-focused approach make this branch a favorite among locals.
